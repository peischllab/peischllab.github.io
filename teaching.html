Lecture 1: Introduction to Bioinformatics

This lecture covers the basics of bioinformatics, including an overview of the field, key concepts, and essential tools and techniques.

Lecture 2: Computational Genomics

This lecture focuses on the computational methods used in genomics, including sequence alignment, genome assembly, and variant calling.

Lecture 3: Machine Learning in Biology

This lecture explores the application of machine learning techniques in biological research, including supervised and unsupervised learning, and their use in analyzing biological data.
