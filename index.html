Welcome to ComBi group @ Interfaculty Bioinformatics Unit, University of Bern

Evolution and Equations

We are part of the Interfaculty Bioinformatics Unit at the University of Bern. Our research focuses on the development of mathematical models and computational tools to study the evolution of biological systems. Our group also organizes and teaches several courses for the Master programm in Bioinformatics and Computational Biology at the University of Bern.

Lab Members

Stephan Peischl, PI

I am using mathematical models and statistical tools to study eco-evolutionary processes.

Karolina Wachala, PhD Student

I leverage statistics and machine learning to investigate eco-evolutionary processes at a genomic resolution.

Ianis Vilela, PhD Student

I am using statistical methods and machine learning algorithms to characterize the environmental factors shaping microbial community composition.

Stefan Struett, PostDoc

I study evolutionary principles, particularly the role of recombination in population genetics.

Ana Hermina Ghenu, Staff Scientist

I design interactive tools that make it easier to learn mathematical modeling in ecology and evolution.
