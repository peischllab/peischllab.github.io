Welcome to ComBi group @Universität Bern

Evolution and Equations

and also Statistics and Machine Learning

We are part of the Interfaculty Bioinformatics Unit at the University of Bern. Our research focuses on the development of mathematical models and computational tools to study the evolution of biological systems. Our group also organizes and teaches several courses for the Master programm in Bioinformatics and Computational Biology at the University of Bern.
